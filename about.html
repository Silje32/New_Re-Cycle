ABOUT US

Re-cycle was founded in 2014 after big complaints from tourists experiencing long bus trips stuck in traffic.

We designed 5 bike trips; east, west, north, south and city, so our tourists get a taste of everything this city has to offer both in history, experiences, nature, food and shops.

We know our town and we think the best way to experience it is by riding a bike with any of our guides.

We trust that our guides will give you the best experience possible.

All of our bikes are eco-friendly so take as many trips you want.

"It´s not about the destination, it´s the journey that is important".
